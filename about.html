Berlin U-bahn Map

This is a dynamic representation of the Berlin U-bahn system, inspired by this map from 2016, but updated to the 2020 namings and routes.

Opened in 1902, the U-Bahn serves 173 stations spread across ten lines, with a total track length of 151.7 kilometres (94.3 mi), about 80% of which is underground.

All the information displayed when clicking on a station (including images) is taken from Wikipedia. Since some of the pages are cached, information might not be in sync with the corresponding wiki page.
